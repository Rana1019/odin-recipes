Bread Omelette

Delicious bread omelette served with fresh chutney

an egg omelette mixture and bread slices

YIt's a delicious and quick breakfast or snack recipe that can be served to kids and mainly it can be given as a school snack or as an evening snack.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • pepper
  • turmeric
  • paprika
  • oil
  • salt

Steps

  1. beat the eggs into a bowl
  2. add salt as per taste, red chilli powder, turmeric powder and black pepper powder.
  3. Stir the mixture well. Beat the mixture well without lumps.
  4. Pour the omelette mixture and spread it well
  5. Take a bread slice and dip it on the omelette mixture and flip the bread and rest it on the omelette.
  6. Doing this way coats the bread with an omelette on both sides
  7. Cook the bread omelette perfectly on both sides on medium flame.
  8. Remove and serve when its golden brown.
